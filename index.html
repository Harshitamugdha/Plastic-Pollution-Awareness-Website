Welcome

Plastic pollution is one of the fastest-growing environmental challenges. From food packaging to shipping materials, plastic waste can travel long distances, breaking into smaller pieces and harming ecosystems along the way. This site is a practical guide for understanding the issue and taking meaningful action.

Explore the science behind plastic pollution, the impacts on wildlife and people, and the solutions that are already working in communities around the world.

Why it matters

  • Everyday convenience, lasting impact: Many plastics are used for minutes but persist for decades.
  • Microplastics are everywhere: When plastic breaks down, tiny particles end up in soil, air, and water.
  • Communities feel the cost: Cleanup efforts, health risks, and lost tourism can strain local economies.
