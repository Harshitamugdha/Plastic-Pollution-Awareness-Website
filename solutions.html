Solutions to Plastic Pollution

Small changes and collective action can drastically reduce plastic leakage into our environment.

Home About Effects Solutions Contact

Everyday actions

  • Choose reusables: Carry a bottle, utensils, and a bag so you can skip disposables.
  • Buy in bulk: Look for refill stations or products with minimal packaging.
  • Sort waste correctly: Clean, dry, and sort recyclables based on local guidelines.
  • Plan before you shop: Make a list to avoid impulse purchases wrapped in plastic.

Community and policy solutions

  • Support local bans: Policies that limit single-use plastics reduce waste at the source.
  • Join cleanups: River and beach cleanups prevent plastic from reaching the ocean.
  • Encourage reuse systems: Advocate for deposit programs and reusable packaging pilots.

Checklist to start today

  1. Swap two single-use items for reusables this week.
  2. Find your city’s recycling guide and post it near the bin.
  3. Talk with a local business about offering refill-friendly options.
  4. Share one action you took to inspire someone else.
